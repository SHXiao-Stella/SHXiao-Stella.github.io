# Welcome! ![](https://visitor-badge.glitch.me/badge?page_id=SHXiao-Stella.readme)

I’m a Master Candidate in the Department of Finance and Investment at Sun Yet-san University.

My primary research interests lie in systemic risk. I've researched how credit asset securitization(CAS) causes systemic risk, how banks escape from the regulation by the cross-holding behavior of CAS, and what is the optimal banking bailout when systemic risk happens. Helping me along the way, I've been learning how to apply recent techniques in machine learning to these practical problems. I also have interests in portfolio selection.
